about

Hi and welcome!

My name is Line Berner, I’m from Copenhagen, and at this moment I'm a Multimedia Design student at KEA. My interests and focus are both in designing and developing web content and solutions. I have always loved the digital and graphic world, and I don't think I can ever get enough.

Before I started at KEA I had never ever seen an HTML file in my life but see where I’m at now only 1,5 years later. I really enjoy getting all this new knowledge and creating stuff with just my bare hands and a little help from my laptop ;)

Currently, I'm on a Frontend Design elective where the focus mainly is on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Here, I really feel that I get into the depth of what JavaScript is capable of and how I can use it in my work. I'm just starting to get into React as well, and this seems to really fit my interest.

As a person, I’m creative, social, and last but not least: Hardworking. I hope you will enjoy my work and if you have any questions or just want to get in contact, please feel free to hit me up. You will find my contact information in the contact section.

Hogwarts Student List

This was a project in the beginning of 3th semester, and was made individually. The assignment was to get data on Hogwarts students and after cleaning the data, make it possible to sort, filter, assign students to different kind of lists, expell and more.

Skills used in this project:

  • HTML, CSS and a lot of JavaScript
  • Rest API
  • Adobe Illustrator

Exam project

This was the final project on my 2th semster at MMD KEA. The assignment was to redesign the webshop of a small start up. And my group choose 'Dolah Soap Bars'. The site was made as a prototype and is not full functional as a real webshop.

My primary role in this project was developing the website and setting up the site in Wordpress.

(Few mistakes/changes can appear on the site due to updates and changes in wordpress over the last few months)

Skills used in this project:

  • Wordpress
  • HTML, CSS and a bit of JavaScript
  • Video and graphic content production (Adobe programs)

Shake it - Game
