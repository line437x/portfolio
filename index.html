about

Hi and welcome!

My name is Line Berner and I’m a Multimedia Design student at KEA. My interests and focus are both on designing and developing web content and solutions. I have always loved the digital and graphic world, and I don't think I can ever get enough.

Before I started at KEA I had never ever seen an HTML file in my life but see where I’m at now only 1,5 years later. I really enjoy getting all this new knowledge and creating stuff with just my bare hands and a little help from my laptop :)

Currently, I'm on a Frontend Design elective where the focus mainly is on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Here, I really feel that I get into the depth of what JavaScript is capable of and how I can use it in my work.

As a person, I’m creative, social, and last but not least: Hardworking. I hope you will enjoy my work and if you have any questions or just want to get in contact, please feel free to hit me up. You will find my information below in the footer and the contact section
